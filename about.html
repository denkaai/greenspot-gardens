The Green Sports Experience

A sanctuary of taste and tranquility in the heart of Kamakis.

Green Sports Gardens is a premier outdoor dining and entertainment destination located in Kamakis, Ruiru along the Eastern Bypass. We offer a unique blend of traditional hospitality and modern garden elegance.

Our vision is to provide a serene escape where master-crafted gastronomy meets the beauty of nature. Whether you're here for our signature grilled platters or an evening of mixology, every moment is designed to be exceptional.

Our Vision

To be the leading garden destination in Kenya, celebrated for excellence in flavor and atmosphere.

Our Mission

To provide a premium, nature-focused environment with world-class service and authentic Kenyan soul.
